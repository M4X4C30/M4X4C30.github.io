Katutubong Tugtugin

Description: The mural is a montage of various indigenous Filipino musical instruments, such as guitars, kulintang, and kudyapi. It highlights these instruments as symbols of Filipino culture, tradition, and identity, often used in celebrations like fiestas, birthdays, and harana.

Title: Katutubong Tugtugin

Artist: Gerilya

Year Created: 2022

Medium/Material Used:Paint on concrete wall

Location: North Pocket Park, Triangle Drive corner 11th Ave, Bonifacio Global City, Philippines

Theme: Cultural Heritage, Filipino Music, Celebration, Identity.

Significance in Filipino Culture:
