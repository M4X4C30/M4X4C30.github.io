About Website

Hiraya, meaning "imagination" in Filipino, is a gallery dedicated to showcasing the vibrant and diverse world of Filipino art. Established to celebrate both emerging and established artists, we bring forth a collection that reflects the rich cultural heritage and creativity of the Philippines. From traditional paintings to modern installations, our mission is to inspire and connect art lovers with the stories behind each masterpiece.

The Team

We are Group 10 from BSIT-1A Web system and tech, a passionate team of students who came together to create Hiraya Filipino Art Gallery as part of our school project. Our goal is to highlight the beauty of Filipino artistry while learning and collaborating as a cohesive unit.
