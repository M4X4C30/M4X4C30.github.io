Fruit Gatherer

Fernando Amorsolo y Cueto





Details

  • Title: Fruit Gatherer
  • Artist: Fernando Amorsolo
  • Year Created: 1950
  • Medium/Material Used: Oil on Canvas
  • Dimension/Size: 41 x 51 cm. (16.1 x 20.1 in.)
  • Location:Auction House

Artwork Description

It depicts a woman sitting under a bamboo tree holding a winnowing basket full of fresh fruits. It also portrays the everyday lives of a lady living in the province where fruits, vegetables, and other edible things can be found fresh

Theme

The painting emphasizes Filipino rural life, hard work, perseverance, and the connection between people and nature. It also highlights the simple joys and struggles of agricultural workers, who rely on the land for survival.
