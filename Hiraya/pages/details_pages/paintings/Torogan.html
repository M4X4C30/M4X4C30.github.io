Torogan

Jose Joya





Details

  • Title: Torogan
  • Artist:Jose Joya
  • Year Created:1975
  • Medium/Material Used: Acrylic on canvas
  • Dimension/Size: 35.5 x 47 inches (90 x 119 cm)
  • Location:Auction House, Leon Gallery, Makati City

Artwork Description

Torogan exemplifies Joya's mastery of color, texture, and form. The painting features broad, layered brushstrokes and spontaneous, intersecting lines that create a sense of movement and rhythm across the canvas. Joya's style in this composition showcases his ability to capture energy and emotion through abstraction, reflecting influences from both Western modernist movements and his Filipino heritage.

Theme

The painting reflects themes of energy, movement, and the essence of the Philippine landscape, capturing the vibrancy and dynamism of nature through abstract forms and bold color choices.

Facts / Trivia

Joya's works have been showcased in various exhibitions, reflecting his prominence in the art community. His paintings are celebrated for their portrayal of Filipino culture and the nuances of daily life.
