Las Virgenes Cristianas Expuestas al Populacho

Félix Resurrección Hidalgo





Details

  • Title: Las Virgenes Cristianas Expuestas al Populacho
  • Artist: Félix Resurrección Hidalgo
  • Year Created: 1884
  • Medium/Material Used: Oil on Canvas
  • Dimension/Size: 1.15 meters by 1.57 meters (approximately 45 inches by 62 inches).
  • Location:Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (Central Bank of the Philippines).

Artwork Description

"Las Virgenes Cristianas Expuestas al Populacho," which translates to "The Christian Virgins Exposed to the Mob," is an oil painting by Filipino artist Félix Resurrección Hidalgo depicting two young Christian women stripped bare and exposed to a leering crowd, highlighting the brutality and sexual violence they face during the Roman persecution of Christians .

Theme

The theme of Las Virgenes Cristianas Expuestas al Populacho by Felix Resurrección Hidalgo revolves around persecution, suffering, and resilience.
