Manpower

Description: Manpower is a mural by Kris Abrigo, a Filipino street artist known for his bold geometric forms and vibrant color palettes. The artwork showcases a dynamic composition of interconnected figures and shapes, symbolizing the collective effort and strength of Filipino workers.

Title: Manpower

Artist: Kris Abrigo

Medium/Material Used:Spray Paint and Acrylic on Concrete

Theme:Labor, Unity, and Collective Strength

Significance in Filipino Culture:

The mural represents the hardworking spirit of the Filipino workforce, emphasizing the importance of laborers and professionals in shaping the nation. It also reflects modern urban life and Filipino adaptability, fostering an appreciation for art in public spaces.

Facts / Trivia:

  • Abrigo is known for his use of geometric figures and bright colors, making his work visually distinctive. His murals are often seen in residential and commercial areas, making street art more accessible to the public.
